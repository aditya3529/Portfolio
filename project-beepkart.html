E-marketplace Implementation

Role: Product Manager Intern Organization: BeepKart Domain: Electric 2 Wheelers Marketplace

Project Overview

The project entailed the end-to-end development of an online marketplace for electric 2 wheelers. This included initial planning, coordination with various teams, and overseeing the complete delivery from inception to completion. Led the entire project lifecycle from initial concept to final launch of Beepkart.com.

Objectives

  • Develop and launch the e-marketplace from scratch
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to create interface mock-ups and prototypes
  • Provide technical leadership to ensure effective Agile development
  • Design and implement user-centric tools such as the Total Cost of Ownership and Savings Calculator

Challenges

  • Managing the technical and functional aspects of the project simultaneously
