Global Automation Enablement of BAU E-commerce Operations

Role: Product Manager Consultant Organization: Thoucentric Client: Leading FMCG Organisation

Project Overview

This project focused on enabling automation for Business-as-Usual (BAU) e-commerce operations at a global scale. With the goal of driving efficiency, scalability, and accuracy across daily workflows, the initiative delivered over 40 production-grade automation bots across regions. The engagement also prioritized strategic alignment with stakeholders, change enablement, and post-deployment adoption.

Objectives

  • Automate repetitive and time-consuming BAU tasks across e-commerce operations
  • Streamline global workflows to reduce operational overhead and human error
  • Deliver scalable automation solutions with high reusability and production readiness
  • Drive stakeholder alignment, secure buy-in, and ensure post-go-live support

Challenges

  • Identifying automation-ready use cases across diverse geographies and functions
  • Ensuring smooth UAT, handover, and post-implementation support for 40+ bots
  • Managing cross-functional collaboration between tech, business, and automation teams
  • Balancing rapid delivery with production-grade quality and governance standards

Solutions Implemented

End-to-End Automation Delivery

  • Delivered 40+ automation bots that optimized various BAU operations such as SKU management, data validation, and listing updates
  • Designed robust workflows that aligned with global operating procedures

Feasibility & Use Case Assessment

  • Led feasibility assessments to identify viable automation opportunities based on ROI and complexity
  • Facilitated UAT processes, ensuring high user satisfaction and minimal post-go-live issues

Stakeholder Alignment & Governance

  • Worked with regional teams, leadership, and IT to align automation efforts with strategic business priorities
  • Secured a 20% project budget increase by demonstrating early wins and strong business case
