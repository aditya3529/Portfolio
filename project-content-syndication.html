Global Content Syndication Automation

Role: Product Manager Consultant Organization: Thoucentric Client: Leading FMCG Organisation

Project Overview

The project aimed to implement an automated content deployment solution to standardize and streamline content syndication processes across 36 retailers in 9 global markets. The initiative sought to enhance operational efficiency, improve content deployment accuracy, and reduce manual errors. The project's success resulted in a 70% improvement in operational efficiency, a 65% increase in content deployment accuracy, a 65% reduction in processing time for e-commerce listing creation, and a 40% decrease in operational costs.

Objectives

  • Standardize the content syndication process across multiple retailers and markets
  • Improve operational efficiency and accuracy in content deployment
  • Automate e-commerce listing creation to reduce processing time and costs
  • Manage stakeholder relationships and define project goals, priorities, and requirements through client proof-of-concept initiatives

Challenges

  • Coordinating content syndication across 36 retailers in diverse global markets
  • Ensuring consistency and accuracy in content deployment amidst varying market requirements
  • Managing and aligning expectations of over 20 stakeholders with different priorities
  • Developing a proof-of-concept that clearly defines project goals and requirements

Solutions Implemented

Automation of Content Deployment

  • Developed an automated solution to handle content syndication processes, ensuring consistent standards across all retailers and markets
  • Integrated advanced algorithms to validate and correct content before deployment, minimizing errors and discrepancies

Process Standardization

  • Established a standardized workflow for content deployment that could be universally applied across different markets and retailers
  • Created comprehensive guidelines and protocols to maintain consistency in content quality and presentation

E-commerce Listing Automation

  • Implemented automation tools for e-commerce listing creation, significantly reducing the time required for processing and enhancing the accuracy of listings
  • Streamlined workflows to enable quicker turnaround times and lower operational costs
