Insurance Policy Compliance Implementation

Role: Senior Product Specialist Organization: HashedIn by Deloitte Client: US Healthcare Organisation

Project Overview

The project aimed to implement NSA (No Surprise Act) policy-compliant attributes to validate insurance categories across a comprehensive health insurance portfolio. This initiative was essential to ensure regulatory compliance, enhance data integrity, and improve customer trust.

Objectives

  • Integrate NSA policy-compliant attributes into the health insurance portfolio
  • Achieve high sprint success rates and deliver high-quality product increments
  • Enhance team velocity and efficiency
  • Develop comprehensive product documentation and support materials

Challenges

  • Ensuring timely integration of compliance attributes across a diverse insurance portfolio
  • Managing and coordinating a cross-functional team effectively
  • Overcoming blockers to maintain project momentum and enhance team productivity
  • Creating and maintaining detailed product documentation to support end-users and stakeholders

Key Contributions

Sprint Management and Success

  • Oversaw sprint ceremonies, including planning, reviews, and retrospectives, to ensure smooth execution and high-quality deliverables
  • Achieved a 90% average sprint success rate by maintaining focus on sprint goals and facilitating effective communication within the team

Team Management

  • Led a cross-functional team of 12 members, including developers, QA engineers, and designers
  • Fostered a collaborative work environment, ensuring all team members were aligned with project objectives and timelines

Enhancing Team Velocity

  • Proactively identified and removed blockers that impeded progress, enabling the team to maintain a steady workflow
  • Conducted bi-weekly product planning sessions to assess issue complexity and sizing, resulting in a 30% increase in team velocity

Product Documentation

  • Developed and updated essential product collaterals, including user manuals, release notes, FAQs, case studies, and demo scripts
  • Ensured comprehensive documentation to support stakeholders and improve user adoption
