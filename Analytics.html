Operational & Behavioural Analytics Implementation

Role: Senior Associate – Change Management Organization: Euronext Solutions Duration: Oct 2024 – Present

Project Overview

The project focused on enhancing operational transparency and user behaviour tracking for a customer portal comprising 14 applications. The implementation introduced advanced KPI dashboards and behavioural analytics, improving performance insights, user journey visibility, and cross-team alignment.

Objectives

  • Enable real-time tracking of performance and adoption KPIs across 14 applications
  • Capture behavioural patterns through heatmaps, funnels, and session recordings
  • Drive data-driven decision-making by integrating operational and behavioural data
  • Reduce manual reporting efforts and ensure stakeholder alignment during change

Challenges

  • Lack of unified visibility into user interactions across distributed apps
  • Manual, time-consuming reporting across operational metrics
  • Resolving data inconsistencies across Amazon Athena queries
  • Driving adoption and understanding of analytics tools across diverse teams

Solutions Implemented

Power BI Dashboard Development

  • Built 14+ Power BI dashboards to track operational KPIs including adoption, performance, and errors
  • Reduced manual reporting time by 50% through automation and scheduled data pulls

Mouseflow Behavioural Analytics Integration

  • Implemented Mouseflow tracking across all 14 apps for heatmaps, funnel analysis, and session recordings
  • Enabled deep insights into user friction points and drop-off trends

Cross-Team Data Collaboration

  • Partnered with 10+ product owners and analysts to define KPIs and optimize tracking
  • Resolved over 15 data discrepancy issues via Amazon Athena queries and schema validation

Change Management & Training Enablement

  • Led communications and training for stakeholders across IT and business units
  • Created documentation and process guides to support onboarding and system transition
